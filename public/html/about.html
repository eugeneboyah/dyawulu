Why Dya-Wulu

Dyawulu Cooperative Learning Center is a dream of Mrs. Ruby T. Martin who has over 25 years of working experience in the Educational Sector. The name Dya-Wulu derive its meaning from the Bassa vernacular which means “Dream/Vision.” The dream came about as a result of Mrs. Ruby T. Martin desire to see quality Christian Pre-School, Primary and Junior High Learning Centers coming to every community in Liberia.

Mrs. Ruby Martin
CEO & Founder DCLC
